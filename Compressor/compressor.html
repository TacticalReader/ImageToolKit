How Image Compression Works

Everything happens in your browser — private & instant.

1
Upload your images Drag & drop or click to browse. You can add up to 10 images at once — PNG, JPG, WebP, AVIF, GIF, BMP, or TIFF. Max 10 MB per file.
2
Choose a compression mode Balanced hits the sweet spot between size and quality. Maximum Savings shrinks files as small as possible. Maximum Quality keeps near-original fidelity with modest savings.
3
Adjust the quality slider Fine-tune compression from 1 % (smallest) to 100 % (largest). The table instantly updates estimated output sizes and savings so you can preview the trade-off before committing.
4
Click "Compress Images" Your browser draws each image onto a hidden canvas and re-encodes it using the Web Canvas API at your chosen quality. No files leave your device — ever.
5
Download your results Each compressed image gets its own download button. Or hit Download All to save everything at once. The status column shows Done when an image is ready.
