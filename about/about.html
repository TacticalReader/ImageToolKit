Why ImageToolkit
exists

ImageToolkit was created to solve a simple problem: preparing and optimizing images and SVGs for the web is usually slow, inconvenient, and full of ads. We built a fast, clean and private toolkit that works entirely in your browser.

Our Mission

To empower developers, designers, and content creators with a set of free, open source tools that make working with images, SVGs and documents faster and easier.

Privacy First

Everything is processed locally in your browser. Your files never leave your device.

Blazing Fast

No uploads. No waiting. Get things done instantly with the power of your own device.

Open Source

Built with ❤️ for the community. Free to use, free to modify, free to share.

Clean & Simple

A clean, distraction-free interface so you can focus on what really matters.

Works Everywhere

Runs on any modern browser, on any device. No installation required.

Made with ❤️ by a developer

ImageToolkit is an independent open source project. I built it to save time during my daily work as a web developer and e-commerce builder.

I hope it helps you too! 🙂
